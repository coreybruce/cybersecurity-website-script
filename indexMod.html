FAQ

Is Aurora Note really free?

Yes. The core app is free. We’ll offer optional Pro features later to support development.

Do you collect analytics?

No usage analytics are collected by default. You can opt‑in to basic crash reporting in Settings → Privacy.

Where is my data stored?

Notes are stored locally in your user profile. If you enable sync, they’re encrypted on your device before upload.
